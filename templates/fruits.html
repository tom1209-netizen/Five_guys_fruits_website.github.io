List of fruits and information about them

Dragon fruits

Dragon fruit has a unique and striking appearance, with a bright pink or yellow outer skin covered in green-tipped scales. The flesh inside can be white or deep magenta, dotted with tiny black seeds, giving it a starry look.

Learn more
Rambutan

Rambutan is a small, round fruit covered in a thick, leathery skin that is bright red or yellow when ripe. The skin is covered in soft, hair-like structures, making it look somewhat hairy. The flesh inside is translucent white, juicy, and clings to a central seed.

Learn more
Mangosteen

The mangosteen has a thick, hard purple rind when ripe. Inside, the fruit is divided into white segments, similar in appearance to garlic cloves but larger and softer. The flesh is juicy and tender.

Learn more
Longan

Longans are small, round fruits with a thin, light brown skin that turns slightly brittle when ripe. The flesh inside is translucent, with a glossy sheen, surrounding a large, shiny black seed, giving it the appearance of an eyeball.

Learn more
Lychee

Lychees have a rough, reddish-pink skin that is easy to peel. The flesh inside is white, translucent, and juicy, encasing a single large, brown seed.

Learn more
Durian

Durian is a large, spiky fruit, with a hard outer shell covered in large, thorn-like spikes. The flesh inside is soft, creamy, and ranges in color from pale yellow to red, depending on the variety.

Learn more
Pomelo

Pomelo is the largest citrus fruit, with a thick, spongy rind that can be green or yellow. The flesh inside is segmented like other citrus fruits and can be pale yellow, pink, or red, with a texture that is firmer than grapefruit.

Learn more
Sapodilla

Sapodilla is a small, egg-shaped fruit with a rough, brown, sandy skin. The flesh inside is soft, grainy, and ranges from pale yellow to brown, with a sweet taste and a texture similar to a ripe pear.

Learn more
Star Apple

Star apple is a round fruit with a smooth skin that can be purple or green. The flesh inside is jelly-like, typically purple or white, and when cut in half, it displays a star pattern formed by the seeds.

Learn more
Passion Fruit

Passion fruit has a round or oval shape with a tough outer rind that is purple or yellow when ripe. The interior is filled with a juicy, orange pulp that is full of small, edible black seeds.
