Longan

Description

Also known as dragon eye fruit, is a tropical fruit native to Southeast Asia. It has a sweet, musky flavor and is related to lychee.

Nutrition facts

(per 100 grams)
- Calories: 60 kcal  
- Protein: 1.31 g  
- Fat: 0.1 g  
- Carbs: 15.14 g  
- Fiber: 1.2 g  
- Vitamin C: 84 mg  
- Iron: 0.13 mg  
- Magnesium: 10 mg

Taste profile

Longan has a sweet, musky flavor, often compared to lychee, with floral and honey notes.
